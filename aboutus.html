MedicalComp. | About Us

MedicalComp was created by our team (CodeX) with the objective of representing and visualising cancer cost treatment data, to help make patients aware of the costs, as well as the range of people it can affect.

Throughout this team project, we have all contributed into making this website to represent the information available about different forms of cancer. Below is the list of our team members and what they contributed.
