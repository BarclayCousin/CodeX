CodeX | About Us

CodeX is a website designed by our team to reppresent and visualise cancer cost treatment data to help make patients aware of the costs it can have, as well as the range of people it can affect.

Throughout this team project, we have all contributed into making this site to represent the information available about different forms of cancer. This site is here to help make people aware.
